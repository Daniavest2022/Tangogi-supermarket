Frequently Asked Questions

What areas do you cover for express delivery?

We currently cover major metropolitan areas within a 15km radius of our stores. Use our address checker to confirm your area.

Is there a minimum order value?

Yes, the minimum order value for express delivery is ₦5,000 to ensure efficient delivery operations.

Can I schedule express delivery in advance?

Yes! You can schedule express delivery up to 7 days in advance for any 2-hour time slot.

What if no one is home during delivery?

Our drivers will attempt contact 3 times. If unavailable, we'll reschedule or you can pick up from our nearest store.
