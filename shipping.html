Shipping Policies

Order Processing

Orders are processed within 1 hour during business hours (8AM - 8PM). Orders placed after 6PM will be processed the next morning.

Restricted Items

Certain items like alcohol, tobacco, and prescription medications have special delivery requirements and may not be available in all areas.

Delivery Attempts

We make 2 delivery attempts. If unsuccessful, items are returned to our warehouse. Redelivery charges may apply for additional attempts.

Perishable Goods

Perishable items are packed with ice packs and delivered in insulated bags to maintain freshness during transit.
