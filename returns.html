What Can Be Returned

  • Unopened and unused items in original packaging
  • Defective or damaged products
  • Wrong items received
  • Missing parts or accessories
  • Expired products
  • Items with quality issues

What Cannot Be Returned

  • Opened perishable goods (unless spoiled)
  • Personal care and hygiene products
  • Gift cards and vouchers
  • Customized or personalized items
  • Items damaged by customer misuse
  • Products without original packaging

Important Note on Perishable Items

Perishable items can only be returned if they are spoiled, expired, or damaged upon delivery. Please contact us within 24 hours of delivery for perishable returns.
