Our Humble Beginnings

How a family dream became Lagos' trusted grocery partner

2020

The Market Stall

Tangogi started as a small family-owned market stall in Anthony Village, Lagos. Our founder, Mrs. Adebayo, began with just fresh tomatoes and peppers.

2021

Digital Transformation

We launched our online platform to serve customers during the pandemic, bringing fresh groceries to doorsteps across Lagos.

2023

Expansion & Growth

Expanded to serve Abuja, Port Harcourt, and Ibadan while partnering with 50+ local farmers and suppliers.

2024

Innovation Leader

Introduced same-day delivery, mobile app, and sustainable packaging initiatives.
