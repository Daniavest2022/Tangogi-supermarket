Ordering Questions

12 questions

To place an order:

  1. Browse products and add items to your cart
  2. Click the cart icon and review your items
  3. Proceed to checkout
  4. Enter your delivery address and contact information
  5. Choose your preferred payment method
  6. Confirm and place your order

You'll receive an order confirmation email with your order details.

You can modify or cancel your order within 30 minutes of placing it. After this period, the order enters our processing system and cannot be changed automatically.

To request changes after 30 minutes:

  • Contact our customer support immediately
  • Call: +234 901 865 1283
  • Use live chat on our website or app
  • Email: support@tangogi.com

There is no minimum order amount for standard delivery. However, for express delivery (2-4 hours), the minimum order is ₦2,000.

Free delivery thresholds:

  • Standard delivery: Free on orders over ₦3,000
  • Express delivery: Free on orders over ₦5,000

To use discount codes:

  1. Add items to your cart and proceed to checkout
  2. Look for the "Discount Code" or "Promo Code" field
  3. Enter your code and click "Apply"
  4. The discount will be automatically applied to your order total

Note: Some codes may have specific terms and conditions, such as minimum order value or product category restrictions.

Delivery & Shipping

15 questions

Delivery Areas:

  • Lagos: All major areas including Victoria Island, Ikoyi, Lekki, Ikeja, Surulere, and more
  • Abuja: Maitama, Asokoro, Wuse, Garki, and surrounding areas
  • Port Harcourt: Select areas with expanding coverage

Delivery Times:

  • Express Delivery: 2-4 hours (available in select areas)
  • Standard Delivery: Same day for orders before 6 PM
  • Next Day Delivery: For areas outside immediate coverage

You can track your order in several ways:

  1. Order Tracking Page: Visit our website and use the order tracking feature
  2. Mobile App: Use the Tangogi app for real-time tracking
  3. SMS Updates: Receive automatic SMS updates at key delivery milestones
  4. Email Notifications: Get detailed email updates with tracking links
  5. Customer Support: Call +234 901 865 1283 for live updates

If you're not available during delivery, our delivery agent will:

  1. Attempt to call you on your registered phone number
  2. Wait for 15 minutes at the delivery location
  3. If possible, leave the package with a neighbor (with your prior permission)
  4. Reschedule delivery for the next available time slot
  5. Return the items to our warehouse after 2 failed attempts

Pro Tip: You can provide delivery instructions in your account settings for safe drop-off locations.

Yes, we deliver 7 days a week, including weekends and most public holidays. However, delivery times may be extended on major public holidays.

Weekend Delivery Schedule:

  • Saturday: Normal delivery hours (8 AM - 8 PM)
  • Sunday: Reduced hours (10 AM - 6 PM)
  • Public Holidays: Limited delivery slots available

We recommend placing orders earlier on weekends and holidays to ensure timely delivery.
