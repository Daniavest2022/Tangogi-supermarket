Frequently Asked Questions

Orders & Delivery

Delivery times vary by location:

  • Lagos Mainland: 2-4 hours
  • Lagos Island: 3-5 hours
  • Other States: 24-48 hours

Express delivery (1-2 hours) is available for select areas at an additional cost.

You can modify your order within 30 minutes of placing it. After this period, the order enters processing and cannot be changed. Contact customer support immediately if you need to make changes.

If you're not available, our delivery agent will:

  1. Attempt to call you twice
  2. Leave the package with a neighbor (if you've given permission)
  3. Reschedule delivery for the next available slot
  4. Return to warehouse after 2 failed attempts (extra charges may apply for redelivery)

Returns & Refunds

We offer a 7-day return policy for most items. Conditions apply:

  • Items must be unopened and in original packaging
  • Perishable goods cannot be returned unless spoiled
  • Proof of purchase required
  • Return shipping is free for defective items

Refund processing times:

  • Credit/Debit Cards: 5-7 business days
  • Bank Transfer: 2-3 business days
  • Wallet Balance: Instant
  • Cash on Delivery Returns: Bank transfer within 3 days
